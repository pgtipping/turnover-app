Guide to Using the Employee Turnover Calculator

Welcome to the guide for the Employee Turnover Calculator. This tool helps you calculate and analyze employee turnover rates over any given period, with monthly data on number of voluntary exits and total employees at the end of each month.

Before using the app, ensure you have the initial or starting headcount for the start month (i.e., total number of employees in the first month of the period which you intend to calculate employee turnover), and monthly employee data for total exits and headcount at monthend.

How to Use the Calculator

To use the calculator, follow these steps:

  1. Enter the initial headcount for the start month in the "Initial Headcount" field.
  2. Select the data entry method (manual or CSV upload).
  3. Select a start month (representing the beginning period) and an end month (representing the end of the period).
  4. Click on the generate form button. This displays a form with four columns, Month, Beginning (prepopulated with your initial headcount and auto updates as you enter the ending headcount), Voluntary Exits, and End (representing ending headcount), for each month. The form is populated with all the months representing your selected period from the start month to the end month.
  5. Enter the number of exits in the "Voluntary Exits" field.
  6. Enter the headcount at monthend in the "End" field. The beginning headcount for the next month dynamically updates to the previous month's ending headcount.
  7. Repeat steps 5 and 6 for each month in the period you wish to analyze.
  8. Select your preferred data for the chart visualization. Options are monthly rates, quarterly rates, year-to-date (YTD) rates, and annualized rates.
  9. Click the "Submit" button to generate the employee turnover rate for each month. A table with the calculated rates, a chart visualization based on selected data, and a generate pdf button will appear below the submit button.
  10. Click the "Generate PDF" button to download a PDF report of the entire page including calculated rates and chart visualization. The generated pdf is automatically saved to your default download folder.
